# SkyNova Airlines Passenger Handbook

## 1. Booking & Reservations

### 1.1 How to Book a Flight
Passengers can book flights via the SkyNova website, mobile app, or by calling our reservations hotline at 1-800-SKY-NOVA. Bookings must be made at least 2 hours before scheduled departure. Group bookings of 10 or more passengers require at least 72 hours advance notice.

### 1.2 Fare Classes
SkyNova offers three cabin classes:
- **Economy**: Standard seating with complimentary snacks and non-alcoholic beverages on flights over 2 hours.
- **Business**: Priority boarding, wider seats with extra legroom, complimentary full meals, and lounge access.
- **First Class**: Lie-flat seats on long-haul routes, gourmet dining, dedicated check-in, and exclusive lounge access.

### 1.3 Booking Changes
Economy tickets may be changed up to 24 hours before departure for a fee of $50 per change. Business and First Class tickets may be changed at no charge up to 2 hours before departure. Same-day changes are subject to seat availability.

### 1.4 Name Corrections
Minor name corrections (up to 3 characters) are allowed free of charge within 24 hours of booking. Full name changes are not permitted — a new ticket must be purchased.

---

## 2. Cancellations & Refunds

### 2.1 Passenger-Initiated Cancellations
- **Economy (non-refundable)**: No cash refund. Travel credit valid for 12 months issued.
- **Economy (flexible)**: Full refund if cancelled more than 24 hours before departure.
- **Business / First Class**: Full refund if cancelled more than 2 hours before departure.

### 2.2 SkyNova-Initiated Cancellations
If SkyNova cancels a flight, passengers are entitled to:
- A full refund to the original payment method within 7 business days, OR
- Rebooking on the next available SkyNova flight at no extra cost, OR
- A travel credit worth 120% of the original ticket value.

### 2.3 Refund Processing Time
Refunds are processed within 7 business days for credit/debit cards and within 20 business days for cash or cheque payments. If a refund has not appeared after these periods, contact support with your booking reference.

### 2.4 Flight Delays
For delays over 3 hours at departure, passengers are entitled to:
- Complimentary meal voucher ($15 value)
- Access to the SkyNova departure lounge (if available)
- Full refund or rebooking if the delay exceeds 5 hours

## 3. Check-In & Boarding

### 3.1 Check-In Windows
- **Online check-in**: Opens 48 hours before departure, closes 2 hours before.
- **Airport check-in**: Opens 3 hours before departure, closes 45 minutes before for domestic and 60 minutes before for international flights.

### 3.2 Boarding Pass
Boarding passes can be downloaded via the SkyNova app, printed at home, or collected at a self-service kiosk. Mobile boarding passes are accepted at all SkyNova-operated airports.

### 3.3 Boarding Order
1. Passengers requiring special assistance
2. First Class passengers
3. Business Class passengers
4. SkyNova Frequent Flyer Platinum & Gold members
5. Economy passengers with Priority Boarding add-on
6. Economy passengers by seat row (rear to front)

### 3.4 Gate Closure
Gates close 15 minutes before departure for domestic flights and 20 minutes before for international. Passengers who miss gate closure forfeit their seat with no refund.

---

## 4. Baggage Policy

### 4.1 Carry-On Baggage
Each passenger is permitted one carry-on bag (max 10 kg, 55×40×23 cm) and one personal item (laptop bag or purse). Oversized or overweight carry-ons will be checked at the gate for a fee of $30.

### 4.2 Checked Baggage Allowance
- **Economy**: 1 bag up to 23 kg included. Additional bags: $40 each.
- **Business**: 2 bags up to 32 kg each included.
- **First Class**: 3 bags up to 32 kg each included.
Overweight fees: $20 per kg over the limit.

### 4.3 Lost or Delayed Baggage
Report lost or delayed baggage at the airport baggage services desk before leaving the arrivals area. A Property Irregularity Report (PIR) will be issued. SkyNova will attempt to locate and deliver delayed baggage within 24 hours. For bags confirmed lost after 21 days, compensation of up to $1,500 is provided for documented losses.

### 4.4 Prohibited Items
The following are not permitted in carry-on or checked baggage: explosives, flammable liquids, lithium batteries over 100Wh, firearms (unless declared and packed per regulations), and perishable items without proper containment.

## 5. Frequent Flyer Program — SkyRewards

### 5.1 Membership Tiers
- **Bronze**: Default tier on enrollment. Earn 1 mile per $1 spent.
- **Silver**: Requires 25,000 tier miles in a calendar year. Earn 1.25 miles per $1. Priority check-in.
- **Gold**: Requires 50,000 tier miles. Earn 1.5 miles per $1. Complimentary seat upgrades (subject to availability). Lounge access on international flights.
- **Platinum**: Requires 100,000 tier miles. Earn 2 miles per $1. Guaranteed seat upgrades. Dedicated customer service line. Complimentary lounge access on all flights.

### 5.2 Redeeming Miles
Miles can be redeemed for flights, seat upgrades, extra baggage, and partner rewards. A minimum of 5,000 miles is required for any redemption. Miles expire after 24 months of account inactivity.

### 5.3 Miles Expiry
Miles earned remain valid as long as the member has account activity (earning or redeeming) at least once every 24 months. Tier status is evaluated annually on December 31.

## 6. Special Assistance

### 6.1 Passengers with Reduced Mobility
SkyNova provides wheelchair assistance, priority boarding, and accessible seating at no additional charge. Requests should be made at least 48 hours before departure by calling our accessibility helpline or noting the requirement during booking.

### 6.2 Unaccompanied Minors
Children aged 5–11 traveling alone must use the SkyNova Unaccompanied Minor (UM) service ($75 each way). Children aged 12–15 may travel alone without the UM service but are encouraged to use it. The service includes escorted boarding, in-flight supervision, and handoff to the designated guardian at the destination.

### 6.3 Special Meals
Special meal requests (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, kosher, etc.) must be submitted at least 24 hours before departure via the Manage Booking portal or by calling reservations. Special meals are available on flights over 2 hours.

---

## 7. In-Flight Services

### 7.1 Wi-Fi
SkyNova offers in-flight Wi-Fi on all Boeing 777 and Airbus A321 aircraft. Pricing: $8 for 1 hour, $15 for the full flight. Business and First Class passengers receive complimentary Wi-Fi on international routes.

### 7.2 Entertainment
Personal seatback screens are available on aircraft operating routes over 3 hours. A selection of movies, TV shows, music, and games is provided at no charge. Passengers may also stream via the SkyNova app on personal devices connected to the onboard Wi-Fi.

### 7.3 Meals & Beverages
Complimentary non-alcoholic beverages are available on all flights. Meals are provided on flights over 4 hours in Economy, and on all flights in Business and First Class. Alcoholic beverages are available for purchase in Economy ($8–$12).

---

## 8. Contact & Support

### 8.1 Customer Support Channels
- **Phone**: 1-800-SKY-NOVA (available 24/7)
- **Email**: support@skynova.com (response within 24 hours)
- **Live Chat**: Available on the website and app between 06:00–22:00 local time
- **Airport Desks**: Open 3 hours before first departure to 1 hour after last arrival

### 8.2 Complaint Resolution
SkyNova aims to resolve all complaints within 14 business days. If unsatisfied with the resolution, passengers may escalate to the Aviation Consumer Authority. Compensation claims for denied boarding, cancellation, or long delays must be submitted within 6 months of the incident.
